Blog Entry #4

During the creation of my portfolio website, I recalled many memories that I preserved through images. I was just looking for a photo of a scenery I had captured before, but then I caught a glimpse of past events in my life.

This image is one of those that reminded me of momens in my life. It was taken during a summer break, when I was just about to become a college student. I was with my childhood best friends, Riza and Roselyn, who are now both in their first year in college. We used to hangout all the time back when we're still in elementary and high school, and this was one of our bonding, secretly riding a motorcycle with Riza guiding us. I tried driving by myself even with my fear of getting into an accident, not because of getting hurt, but because none of my parents knew that I was outside and doing a dangerous act. They didn’t want me to learn to ride a motorcycle because they thought it was dangerous, but my stubborn younger self did the opposite. Even so, I never truly had the chance to learn how to ride properly. Luckily, we didn't get into an accident that time; rather, we had a lot of fun and enjoyed our time together.

But I didn't know that it would be the last time I could try to ride a motorcycle because we can't ride together anymore. Even though we're neighbors and we literally just have to walk a few steps to get to each other's houses, we never really have time to hangout now. We're all busy with our own lives, and we only get the chance to see each other once in blue moon. Luckily, we were able to go out together last January 8, because of Riza's birthday, which was a year after our last hangout with all the three of us present. Because of conflicting schedules, we rarely get to be together as a complete group. With this simple image, I recalled a lot of memories and the emotions I had at that time. It made me miss them more, and I hope we see each other again soon.

Another memory that I recalled was just last year. I saw the images that I captured using the iPad. It was the time when my phone broke and I didn't have a replacement so I had to use my sister's iPad, bringing it outside even if it's too big and heavy. I remembered how unfamiliar the vibe was during that time, when I had to carry that big iPad, to take pictures or watch videos, which would really catch someone else's attention. I know it's normal to use an iPad, but it's really weird and unfamiliar for me because that was the first time in my life that I had to use an iPad for my daily activities.

It felt even weirder because I lost access to my Instagram and Facebook accounts. It really frustrated me because I treasure those accounts. They are one of the ways I can relive moments of my life. I no longer have access to those social media accounts and I had to make new ones. Since then, my life has been different, quiet but not peaceful. I felt like I was away from a few people. My life have been a little quieter because my social media accounts are empty. That's when I realized how dependent I had become on the Internet, I realize how my world revolved around it.

Although it may have negative effects on me, I am thankful for having technology because it is also a way to preserve and relive moments from my life. Since I lost my phone, I didn't have the chance to recover some of the photos I've kept there. I forgot to upload them to my Gdrive, where I keep the important images I want to save. From now on, whenever there's something to keep, I immediately upload it to Gdrive. I am thinking of other ways to preserve my photos, but this one works good for me.

During that timeline of my life — losing my phone and had to use an iPad, losing access to my social media accounts and making new ones, and forgetting to upload some of my cherished moments to Gdrive, then learning to always upload them from now on — I experienced so many emotions and that it almost felt strange. Those images that I captured using the iPad are all here on my current device and also uploaded to the Gdrive. And those images that I forgot to upload, I guess I'll just have to recall them through my own memory.

Interest Blog

Scenery

Sometimes, the simplest moments bring the deepest sense of peace. In a world full of noise, responsibilities, and endless thoughts, finding a place where the mind can rest feels truly priceless. For me, that sense of comfort comes from witnessing breathtaking views — quiet, beautiful scenes that allow me to pause, breathe, and feel free from the weight of my worries.

Games

Game is not just a game; it is an experience filled with emotions, learning, and interaction. It brings people together, allowing everyone to meet, connect, and share moments with one another. More than simple entertainment, games create memories, friendships, and stories that stay with us even after the screen is turned off.

Memories

Memorable moments in life are meant to be treasured and remembered. We never know what experiences await us in the future, but it is important to value what we have already encountered. Even the simple things we accomplish help shape who we are today and who we will become in the future.

Diary

Events in our lives, whether they are big and unforgettable or small and simple, can always be written down in a diary. For me, keeping a diary is something meaningful and comforting. It allows me to capture moments that might otherwise fade with time. Even the ordinary days—when nothing extraordinary seems to happen—become special once they are written on paper, because they represent a part of my journey.

Writing in my diary is both enjoyable and therapeutic. It provides a safe space where I can openly share my thoughts, feelings, and experiences without worrying about being judged. At the end of each day, I like to sit down and reflect on everything that happened—what made me smile, what challenged me, and what lessons I gained. Through this habit of writing, I learn more about myself and become more aware of my emotions.

I often reread what I have written in my diary. When I do, I am reminded of the person I was during those times. It feels like revisiting a younger version of myself, seeing how much I have grown and changed. Sometimes I laugh at my old thoughts, and sometimes I feel emotional remembering moments I had forgotten. My diary becomes a time capsule of my life, preserving memories that I can look back on whenever I want to reflect on my journey.

Music

Music plays a major role in everyday life. It is not only a popular form of leisure but also helps with many daily activities, including traveling, exercising, and focusing on intellectual work. People listen to music for enjoyment, and also to influence or enhance their moods.

Every beat in music gives off different vibes and emotions. I listen to music in many ways depending on how I feel. When I’m sad, I play slow or melancholic songs so I can feel that someone else shares my emotions. I also listen to upbeat music to lift my spirits, helping me forget my worries and simply enjoy the rhythm. When I'm happy, I choose lively songs to feel more productive and energetic. I also love listening to love songs—they make me feel the emotions of love and imagine what it would be like to be deeply in love, even though I haven’t experienced it yet. Love songs make the world feel beautiful, reminding me of the people I cherish and care for.

I have been organizing my music playlists on Spotify so it's easier for me to choose what kind of music I want to listen to. I enjoy exploring different genres, so much that I don't even sure if I have a favorite. I listen to anything that catches my interest, letting my mood guide my choices.

I don't only love listening to music through my devices, but also live. Whenever I have the chance to attend a free concert in our city, I make sure to grab the opportunity and fully enjoy the moment. I love hearing live vocals because I can feel the artist's emotions through the microphone. Being in the same space as the performers allows me to experience the music more deeply and truly connect with each lyric and rhythm.

I have always dreamed of attending a concert by famous bands and immersing myself in their live performance. Since I can’t experience it yet, I hold onto the hope that I will someday fulfill that dream. It serves as an inspiration to keep going and work toward my goals. Everything I want to have or experience in the present becomes motivation, reminding me that I can make these dreams a reality in the future.
